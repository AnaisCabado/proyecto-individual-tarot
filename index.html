HOW TO READ TAROT

SINGLE CARD READING (DAILY GUIDANCE)

This is a simple and quick method, ideal for daily insight or answering a straightforward question.

  1. Shuffle the deck while focusing on your question or intention.
  2. Draw one card from the deck.
  3. Interpret the card based on its meaning and how it relates to your situation.
  4. Consider its upright or reversed position for additional nuance.

FIVE CARD SPREAS (DEEPER INSIGHT AND GUIDANCE)

Provides a more detailed reading, often used for complex situations.

  1. Shuffle the deck while concentrating on your question.
  2. Draw five cards and place them in a spread.

  4. Consider patterns, elements, and card reversals for a deeper interpretation.
